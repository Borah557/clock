About Us

Where Craft Meets Cuisine

Welcome to ABACUS BREWING CO. & KITCHEN

Located in the vibrant heart of Guwahati, ABACUS BREWING CO. & KITCHEN is your premier destination for exceptional craft beer and innovative cuisine. We combine traditional brewing techniques with modern culinary artistry to create an unforgettable dining experience.

Our passionate team crafts each beer with precision and serves dishes that perfectly complement our unique brews. Join us for an experience that celebrates the art of brewing and the joy of great food.
